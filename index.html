Turtle business

Did you know turtles are dieing at an alarming rate? Mostly from plastic pollution and illegal hunting. People also usually hunt them a lot and they do it illegaly decreasing the population of turtles to almost extinct. Hopefully in the future people that do these things get arrested and we slowly stop using plastic :)
